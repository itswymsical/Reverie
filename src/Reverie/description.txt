Reverie is an Open-World RPG Sandbox; it introduces a large storyline, channeled through a mainline mission/quest system, complimented by side missions, new gameplay mechanics such as Leveling, Skill Trees, Cutscences & Dialogue, etc.

The story of Reverie revolves around the Hero, who mysteriously crash-lands into the house of Laine, the Guide. Laine quickly realizes that the Hero's arrival is connected to an ancient power known as Reverie, which is wielded by chosen heroes and linked to the world's forgotten history.
