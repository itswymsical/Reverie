Project Reverie is a content-based mod that introduces an adventure RPG format into Terraria's sandbox formula. By introducing a variety of Missions, Interactive NPCs, Mini-Boss hunts & subworlds, Reverie adds "Looter Shooter" elements into Terraria's formula.
